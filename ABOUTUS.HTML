ABOUT US

University of Gondar Hospital is one of the best hospitals in Ethiopia, which gives a number of better services for the society.the hospital has a number of medical and administrative staffs. These staff serves a population of four million across the region. As a university hospital, it plays an important role in teaching medical and nursing students. Hospital Management System is management system which is used to manage the hospital,we are explaining the various functionality of hospital management system. The existing system in university of Gondar hospital has no advanced computerized system and they have no online registration and patient information recording system.Soit is difficult to treat a number of patients which visit the hospital. Besides, it takes a lot of time to retrieve the patient record and register a number of patients whose serve at Gondar hospital. Those are the core problems we have already dug out from the hospital and propose automated hospital management system which helps to solve the above problem
